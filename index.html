Short Bio

The Essence is the cinematic rock/metal project of multi‑instrumentalist Tim Pelzer from the Calgary area. Launched in 2019, The Essence pairs hard‑hitting sonics with unapologetically Christian themes, aiming for excellence without compromise. Drawing on influences like Red, Skillet, Chevelle, Muse, Starset, Disciple, and Hans Zimmer, Tim crafts songs to challenge and edify listeners. Debut album Caverns and new single Entropy showcase his vision for high‑impact, faith‑forward rock.

Genre: Cinematic Rock / Metal Location: Calgary, AB For press: contact@theessence.com

About The Essence

The Essence is a passion project started by Tim Pelzer in 2019 to create quality Christian music that both challenges and edifies. Rather than watering things down, Tim pursues Christ‑honoring lyrics and cinematic rock production—believing excellent, uncompromising art connects with more people than we expect. Shaped by Scripture, real‑life trials, and influences like Red, Skillet, Chevelle, Muse, Starset, Disciple, Love & Death, Silent Planet, and Hans Zimmer, The Essence delivers hard‑hitting songs with a hard‑hitting message.

Debut album Caverns collects years of writing and refining; the new single Entropy continues that trajectory. No filler—only work crafted with intention and scrutiny.
